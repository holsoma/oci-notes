Templates

  • Collaboration with OML template UI by
    • sharing work
    • publishing work as reports
    • creating notebooks form templates
  • you can store your notebooks as templates, share your notebooks and provide sample templates to other users

Types of Templates

  • Personal
    • notebook templates you created
  • Shared
    • you can share notebook templates with all authenticated users
  • Example
    • pre-populated OML notebook templates

Sharing a template

  • you must create your own template first (save existing notebook as template)
  • saved as shared template for collaboration

Creating Notebook from Templates

  • avoid redoing steps
  • create a notebook from a personal template with “Create Notebook”
  • you can share any of your personal templates, and provide a new name to the shared template
  • when seeing a shared template, you can
    • create a notebook from the template
    • edit, or delete it
  • under shared templates, you can share your notebook with all authenticated OML users
  • you can also collaborate by exporting your notebook as a JSON, and this JSON file can be imported into the same or a different environment
